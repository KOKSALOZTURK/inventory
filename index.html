Welcome!

This app helps you track your IT equipment inventory using QR codes. Easily add, assign, and track assets for your organization. Works on both mobile and tablet devices.

How to Use

  1. Add new equipment with name, ID, user, and assignment date.
  2. Generate a unique QR code for each equipment item.
  3. Print and attach the QR code to the equipment.
  4. Scan QR codes to check equipment in or out, or to update assignment.
  5. View and manage your inventory from any device.
